About

ThermoSecure™ by HouseCom is an all-in-one hub that lets users control everything from the temperature to the security of their home. What started as a passion project for the founders has quickly become Canada’s most reliable home control system. Providing comfort and security in one easy-to-use product provides peace of mind and easy control.

Features

Home security check with smart lock and camera system.

Controlled with Android and iOS from anywhere using a smartphone, tablet or apple watch.

Saves up energy by controlling heat and cooling levels

Updates current weather & time with independent room temperature, humidity and air quality sensing.

Sleek wireless capsule design which fits almost everywhere.
